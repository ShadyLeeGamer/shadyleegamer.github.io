ARrow Simulator

    Currently working on a local multiplayer game experience using AR Foundation. AR features include plane detection, entity placement and anchoring.

    You can play with up to 4 friends and trajectory is drawn to assist with aiming.

    Also implemented a item spawner that keeps track of plane bounds to spawns power ups nearby into the field.
