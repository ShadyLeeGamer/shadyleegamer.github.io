Tap Fever

    This was my first published arcade game on Google Play Store at age 12. It reported 89 unique installs.

    TAP FEVER

    The gameplay was inspired by Fruit Ninja which tests your reflexes in slicing those fruits while avoiding the bombs. In this game, you have to tap as many shrinking circles as possible before they disappear and dodge the bombs. And as time passes, the gameplay speeds up so it gets harder.

    The game features 3 different skins for customisation. All graphics were generated in GIMP.

    There are also 4 additional game modes for added variety.
