About me

Seasoned Unity Developer with 7 years of XP with passion for making games since the age of 10. My intense curiosity for games and the creative problem-solving they demand has led to a diverse portfolio of collaborative and independent projects. Notably, I published an exciting high-speed anime platformer on Itch.io featuring gravity-bending physics.

My greatest back-end experience was when I led the transition of a multiplayer card game from Alpha to Closed Beta and launched a testing campaign through web dev, which attracted over 20 players resulting in valuable user feedback and live testing of these systems. This included: Realtime Database, Authentication, Cloud Storage and Hosting.

Having built various mobile games from my very first arcade game at the age of 14 to a noteworthy survival game made during my BTEC course, I am very confident with mobile game development. Now, I am making AR experiences for Android and PC alongside artists, designers and fellow devs.

Had lots of fun participating in over 13 game jams, including one that ranked 15th place out of 158 entries!

I am eager to take on new challenges.
